About Us

About Dillard Pools

Dillard Pools was started in 2003 with a vision and desire of providing friendly quality pool service with integrity.

I’m sure you’ve seen a street sign saying “Drive like your kids live here.” At Dillard Pools we service your pool like our kids swim in it. Our utmost desire is that you are safe to enjoy your pool. We understand the fear and anxiety that comes with being unsure whether or not the water chemistry of the pool is balanced for you and your friends and family to swim. We are here to provide you with pool service that gives you that peace of mind so you can stop worrying about chemistry and start having an amazing time in your safe and sparkling pool!

With over 35 years combined experience, we’ve had the pleasure of helping hundreds of people swim for thousands of hours. And we would love to be able to help you too! With all of the demands of life on your busy plate, your pool should be a place of fun and relaxation – not a chore for you to work on! We know it can be a daunting task trying to figure out how to take good care of the pool by yourself. We are happy to look after your pool so you can spend your valuable time taking care of the things that matter most to you.

So give us a call today and see just how stress-free having a pool that is always ready for swimming really can be. We can’t wait to get your pool in the perfect shape and keep it that way. In the end, you’ll find yourself as so many others have, not looking back – only looking forward to the next time you get to dive into your beautiful pool to make incredible memories with your friends and family.

We can’t wait to help you get your pool from questionable to absolutely beautiful!
